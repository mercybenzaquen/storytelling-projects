About Me

About pages are awkward

At first, I just wanted to write. Then I learned to use cameras, lights, editing software, and wanted to be a docummentary filmmaker. I also spent some time doing news photography. A few years later, I spend the fall shadowing a news producer. That same year, I put my camera down for a while to produced the Spanish language newscast of my school. It wasn't until my Senior year in college that I fell for data. Now I spend hours cleaning data and visualizing it. I have tried and learned from each corner of the jornalism field. And I love it.
